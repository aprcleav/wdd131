Explore April's Creations

Tucked away in the misty forests of the Pacific Northwest, April Cleaver spends most days sketching on an iPad and turning everyday adventures into colorful illustrations. When not drawing, she's probably hiking a trail, making questionable sounds on a new musical instrument, or laughing with family. Her art is fueled by curiosity, a love of nature, and the joy of experimenting—whether that's with brush strokes or guitar strings.
